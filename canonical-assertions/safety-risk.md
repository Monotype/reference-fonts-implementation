# Safety, Risk, and Enforcement

## Fonts distributed through Monotype platforms are vetted for security and quality
Fonts made available through Monotype platforms are vetted for quality and security prior to release.

## Monotype does not perform routine or automated audits of customers
Monotype does not routinely or systematically audit customers’ font usage.

## Monotype prioritizes education and collaboration over punitive enforcement
When font misuse is identified, Monotype emphasizes remediation and education rather than punitive enforcement.

## Font misuse is evaluated against license terms, regardless of intent
Use outside the scope of a license constitutes infringement whether intentional or accidental.

## Font licenses must remain valid for the duration of use
Licenses must remain active for as long as fonts are deployed or distributed.
